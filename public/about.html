About Me

I am a Self obsessed , Enigmatic and Selfish individual who aims to hear good things about himself and not work on his imperfections.

I am writing all of this to seek modesty from myself and make the world around me realise how fully self aware i am about myself .

I try to remain calm and sane but it never actually works because overthinking about illogical things and things that are uncontrollable is my passion .
